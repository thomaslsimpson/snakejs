SNAKE (With Abuse Mode)

Snake is a simple game. Using the cursor keys, direct your snake to eat the yellow blocks. Avoid everything else. (Even yourself, except in Easy mode.)

Difficulty
Higher difficulty means a higher score. But watch out for the extra blocks that put in your path.

Controls

  • SPACE BAR: Paused the game.
  • Cursor keys: move the snake.
  • Toggle Fullscreen Button: change into and out of full screen mode.
  • Block Wide/High: change the number of blocks on the field.
